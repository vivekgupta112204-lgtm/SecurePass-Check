About SecurePass Check

Our Mission

SecurePass Check was built with a simple goal: To make the internet safer for everyone, specifically students and non-technical users.

We noticed that many password tools are too technical, cluttered with ads, or simply untrustworthy. We wanted to create a tool that is clean, educational, and strictly privacy-first.

Educational Purpose

This tool is designed for educational purposes. It helps users understand the mathematical relationship between password complexity and security.

Methodology

We use standard brute-force calculation metrics assuming an attack speed of 1 billion guesses per second. This is a standard benchmark in the cybersecurity industry.

SecurePass Check is a free utility provided to the community.
