Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

Welcome to SecurePass Check. We prioritize your privacy above all else. This policy explains how we handle data when you use our password strength checking tool.

2. No Password Storage

We do not collect, store, or transmit your passwords.

The password strength calculation is performed entirely on your device (Client-Side) using JavaScript. When you type a password into SecurePass Check, it never leaves your web browser. It is not sent to any server, database, or third party.

3. Data Collection

We do not collect personal identification information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

However, we may use third-party services that collect anonymous usage data:

  • Google Analytics: We may use Google Analytics to understand website traffic (e.g., number of visitors, country of origin). This data is anonymized.

4. Cookies & Advertisements

We use third-party vendors, including Google, to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to SecurePass Check and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Contact page.
