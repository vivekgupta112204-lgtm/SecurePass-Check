1. Zero-Knowledge Architecture

SecurePass Check is a client-side application. This is the most important part of our policy. When you type a password into the input field on our homepage, the analysis code runs inside your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.). Your password data is never sent to our servers, is never stored in a database, and is never seen by the website owners.

2. Data Collection

While we do not collect your passwords, we use third-party tools to maintain the website:

  • Log Files: Like many other websites, we utilize log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks. This information is not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

3. Google AdSense & DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is a third-party vendor on our site. It uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to www.website.com and other sites on the internet.

  • Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

4. Links to External Sites

Our website may contain links to external sites that are not operated by us. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy and terms and conditions of every site you visit.
